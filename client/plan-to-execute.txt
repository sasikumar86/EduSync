EXECUTIVE SUMMARY FOR SCHOOL DEMO
Project Name: EduSync - Enterprise School Management System

Vision: A comprehensive, multi-tenant SaaS platform enabling schools to digitize all operations including student management, academics, financials, communications, and digital learning—accessible via Web, Android, and macOS using Anti-Gravity IDE.

Key Demo Features:

Real-time attendance and grade tracking
AI-powered chatbot for instant school queries
Individual student/parent/staff portals with digital library
Integrated fee management with payment gateway
Cross-platform accessibility (Web/Android/macOS)
Built entirely with Anti-Gravity IDE low-code/no-code approach
